双曲函数性质总结
2019-12-16|其他
字数总计: 1.5k|阅读时长: 8 分钟

总结一下双曲函数的性质, 方便以后复习用.


定义

shx=ex−e−x2\text{sh} x = \frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{2} shx=2ex−e−x​

chx=ex+e−x2\text{ch} x = \frac{e^x+e^{-x}}{2} chx=2ex+e−x​

thx=ex−e−xex+e−x\text{th} x = \frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{e^x+e^{-x}} thx=ex+e−xex−e−x​

cthx=ex+e−xex−e−x\text{cth} x = \frac{e^x+e^{-x}}{e^x-e^{-x}} cthx=ex−e−xex+e−x​

sechx=2ex+e−x\text{sech} x = \frac{2}{e^x+e^{-x}} sechx=ex+e−x2​

cschx=2ex−e−x\text{csch} x = \frac{2}{e^x-e^{-x}} cschx=ex−e−x2​

作为对照:

sin⁡x=eix−e−ix2i=−ish(ix)\sin x = \frac{e^{ix}-e^{-ix}}{2i} = -i\text{sh}(ix) sinx=2ieix−e−ix​=−ish(ix)

cos⁡x=eix+e−ix2=ch(ix)\cos x = \frac{e^{ix}+e^{-ix}}{2} = \text{ch}(ix) cosx=2eix+e−ix​=ch(ix)

另外，对于反双曲函数有等价形式:

arsinhx=ln⁡∣x+x2+1∣\text{arsinh}x = \ln \vert x + \sqrt{x^2+1} \vert arsinhx=ln∣x+x2+1​∣

arcoshx=ln⁡∣x+x2−1∣\text{arcosh}x = \ln \vert x + \sqrt{x^2-1} \vert arcoshx=ln∣x+x2−1​∣

artanhx=12ln⁡(1+x1−x)\text{artanh}x = \frac{1}{2} \ln \left( \frac{1+x}{1-x} \right) \\ artanhx=21​ln(1−x1+x​)

arcothx=12ln⁡(x+1x−1)\text{arcoth}x = \frac{1}{2} \ln \left( \frac{x+1}{x-1} \right) \\ arcothx=21​ln(x−1x+1​)


基本公式

ch2x+(−sh2x)=1\text{ch}^2x + (-\text{sh}^2x) = 1 \\ ch2x+(−sh2x)=1

(−th2x)=sech2x−1(-\text{th}^2x) = \text{sech}^2x - 1 \\ (−th2x)=sech2x−1

(−cth2x)=(−csch2x)−1(-\text{cth}^2x) = (-\text{csch}^2x) - 1 \\ (−cth2x)=(−csch2x)−1


两角和差公式

sh(x±y)=shx⋅chy±chx⋅shy\text{sh} (x \pm y) = \text{sh} x \cdot \text{ch} y \pm \text{ch} x \cdot \text{sh} y sh(x±y)=shx⋅chy±chx⋅shy

ch(x±y)=chx⋅chy∓(−shx⋅shy)\text{ch} (x \pm y) = \text{ch} x \cdot \text{ch} y \mp (- \text{sh} x \cdot \text{sh} y) ch(x±y)=chx⋅chy∓(−shx⋅shy)

th(x±y)=thx±thy1∓(−thx⋅thy)\text{th} (x \pm y) = \frac{\text{th}x \pm \text{th} y}{1 \mp (-\text{th}x \cdot \text{th} y)} th(x±y)=1∓(−thx⋅thy)thx±thy​


sh(2x)=2shx⋅chx\text{sh}(2x) = 2\text{sh}x \cdot \text{ch}x \\ sh(2x)=2shx⋅chx

ch(2x)=ch2x−(−sh2x)=2ch2x−1=1−(−2sh2x)\text{ch}(2x) = \text{ch}^2 x - (-\text{sh}^2 x) = 2 \text{ch}^2 x - 1 = 1 - (-2\text{sh}^2 x) \\ ch(2x)=ch2x−(−sh2x)=2ch2x−1=1−(−2sh2x)

th(2x)=2thx1−(−th2x)\text{th}(2x) = \frac{2\text{th}x}{1-(-\text{th}^2x)} \\ th(2x)=1−(−th2x)2thx​


sh(3x)=shx⋅(3−(−4sh2x))\text{sh}(3x) = \text{sh} x \cdot (3 - (-4\text{sh}^2x)) \\ sh(3x)=shx⋅(3−(−4sh2x))

ch(3x)=chx⋅(4ch2x−3)\text{ch}(3x) = \text{ch} x \cdot (4\text{ch}^2x - 3) \\ ch(3x)=chx⋅(4ch2x−3)


(−sh2x)=ch(2x)−12(-\text{sh}^2x) = \frac{\text{ch}(2x) - 1}{2} \\ (−sh2x)=2ch(2x)−1​

ch2x=ch(2x)+12\text{ch}^2x = \frac{\text{ch}(2x) + 1}{2} \\ ch2x=2ch(2x)+1​

thx=sh(2x)1+ch(2x)=sh(x)⋅(1−ch(2x))(−sh2(2x))=ch(2x)−1sh(2x)\text{th}x = \frac{\text{sh}(2x)}{1+\text{ch}(2x)} = \frac{\text{sh}(x) \cdot (1-\text{ch}(2x))}{(-\text{sh}^2(2x))} = \frac{\text{ch}(2x)-1}{\text{sh}(2x)} \\ thx=1+ch(2x)sh(2x)​=(−sh2(2x))sh(x)⋅(1−ch(2x))​=sh(2x)ch(2x)−1​


积化和差与和差化积

shx⋅chy=12(sh(x+y)+sh(x−y))\text{sh}x \cdot \text{ch}y = \frac{1}{2}\big(\text{sh}(x+y)+\text{sh}(x-y)\big) \\ shx⋅chy=21​(sh(x+y)+sh(x−y))

chx⋅shy=12(sh(x+y)−sh(x−y))\text{ch}x \cdot \text{sh}y = \frac{1}{2}\big(\text{sh}(x+y)-\text{sh}(x-y)\big) \\ chx⋅shy=21​(sh(x+y)−sh(x−y))

chx⋅chy=12(ch(x+y)+ch(x−y))\text{ch}x \cdot \text{ch}y = \frac{1}{2}\big(\text{ch}(x+y)+\text{ch}(x-y)\big) \\ chx⋅chy=21​(ch(x+y)+ch(x−y))

(−shx⋅shy)=12(ch(x+y)−ch(x−y))(-\text{sh}x \cdot \text{sh}y) = \frac{1}{2}\big(\text{ch}(x+y)-\text{ch}(x-y)\big) \\ (−shx⋅shy)=21​(ch(x+y)−ch(x−y))


shx+shy=2shx+y2⋅chx−y2\text{sh} x + \text{sh} y = 2 \text{sh} \frac{x+y}{2} \cdot \text{ch} \frac{x-y}{2} \\ shx+shy=2sh2x+y​⋅ch2x−y​

shx−shy=2chx+y2⋅shx−y2\text{sh} x - \text{sh} y = 2 \text{ch} \frac{x+y}{2} \cdot \text{sh} \frac{x-y}{2} \\ shx−shy=2ch2x+y​⋅sh2x−y​

chx+chy=2chx+y2⋅chx−y2\text{ch} x + \text{ch} y = 2 \text{ch} \frac{x+y}{2} \cdot \text{ch} \frac{x-y}{2} \\ chx+chy=2ch2x+y​⋅ch2x−y​

chx−chy=(−2shx+y2⋅shx−y2)\text{ch} x - \text{ch} y = (-2 \text{sh} \frac{x+y}{2} \cdot \text{sh} \frac{x-y}{2}) \\ chx−chy=(−2sh2x+y​⋅sh2x−y​)


微分与积分

d(shx)=chx  dx\text{d}(\text{sh}x) = \text{ch} x\;\text{d}x \\ d(shx)=chxdx

(−d(chx))=−shx  dx(-\text{d}(\text{ch}x)) = -\text{sh}x\;\text{d}x \\ (−d(chx))=−shxdx

d(thx)=sech2x  dx\text{d}(\text{th}x) = \text{sech}^2 x\;\text{d}x \\ d(thx)=sech2xdx

d(cthx)=−csch2x  dx\text{d}(\text{cth}x) = -\text{csch}^2 x\;\text{d}x \\ d(cthx)=−csch2xdx

(−d(sechx))=shxch2x=tanhx⋅sechx  dx(-\text{d}(\text{sech}x)) = \frac{\text{sh} x}{\text{ch}^2x} = \text{tanh}x \cdot \text{sech} x\;\text{d}x \\ (−d(sechx))=ch2xshx​=tanhx⋅sechxdx

d(cschx)=−chxsh2x=−cothx⋅cschx  dx\text{d}(\text{csch}x) = -\frac{\text{ch}x}{\text{sh}^2x} = -\text{coth} x \cdot \text{csch}x\;\text{d}x \\ d(cschx)=−sh2xchx​=−cothx⋅cschxdx

(−∫shx  dx)=−chx\left(-\int \text{sh} x \;\text{d}x\right) = -\text{ch} x \\ (−∫shxdx)=−chx

∫chx  dx=shx\int \text{ch} x \;\text{d}x = \text{sh} x \\ ∫chxdx=shx

d(arsinhx)=11−(−x2)  dx\text{d}(\text{arsinh}x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-(-x^2)}}\;\text{d}x \\ d(arsinhx)=1−(−x2)​1​dx

d(arcoshx)=i⋅−11−x2  dx=1x2−1  dx\text{d}(\text{arcosh}x) = i \cdot -\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\;\text{d}x = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2-1}} \;\text{d}x \\ d(arcoshx)=i⋅−1−x2​1​dx=x2−1​1​dx

d(artanhx)=11+(−x2)  dx\text{d}(\text{artanh}x) = \frac{1}{1+(-x^2)}\;\text{d}x \\ d(artanhx)=1+(−x2)1​dx

∫arsinhx  dx=x⋅arsinhx−1−(−x2)\int \text{arsinh}x \;\text{d}x = x \cdot \text{arsinh}x - \sqrt{1-(-x^2)} \\ ∫arsinhxdx=x⋅arsinhx−1−(−x2)​

∫arcoshx  dx=x⋅arcoshx−ix2−1\int \text{arcosh}x \;\text{d}x = x \cdot \text{arcosh}x - i\sqrt{x^2-1} \\ ∫arcoshxdx=x⋅arcoshx−ix2−1​

∫artanhx  dx=x⋅artanhx−12ln⁡∣1+(−x2)∣\int \text{artanh}x \;\text{d}x = x \cdot \text{artanh}x - \frac{1}{2}\ln\big\vert 1+(-x^2) \big\vert \\ ∫artanhxdx=x⋅artanhx−21​ln∣∣​1+(−x2)∣∣​


总结

由于受到sin⁡x=−ish(ix)\sin x = -i\text{sh}(ix)sinx=−ish(ix)的影响, 等式出现sh2x\text{sh}^2xsh2x, d(chx)\text{d}(\text{ch}x)d(chx)的时候, 等式需要变号. 间接包含例如csch2x\text{csch}^2xcsch2x, th2x\text{th}^2xth2x, cth2x\text{cth}^2xcth2x与d(sechx)\text{d}(\text{sech}x)d(sechx)也要变号. 反双曲函数求导/积分过程中, 与arcosh\text{arcosh}arcosh无关的情况x2x^2x2变号, 与arcosh\text{arcosh}arcosh有关的情况含有根号的项整体乘 iii .

(以上为危险言论, 后果自负)
